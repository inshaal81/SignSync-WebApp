How SignSync Works

What is SignSync?

SignSync is a web application that uses machine learning to recognize American Sign Language (ASL) hand gestures. It can identify the 26 letters of the ASL alphabet from images captured via your webcam.

The Technology

SignSync uses a trained neural network model to classify hand gestures. When you take a snapshot, the image is sent to our server where it's processed and analyzed by the model. The model returns its prediction along with a confidence score indicating how certain it is about the result.

Step-by-Step Process

1

Capture

Your webcam captures an image of your hand gesture.

2

Process

The image is sent to our server and preprocessed for the model.

3

Classify

Our ML model analyzes the image and identifies the ASL letter.

4

Result

The predicted letter and confidence score are displayed to you.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting so your hand is clearly visible
  • Position your hand against a plain, contrasting background
  • Keep your hand steady when taking a snapshot
  • Make sure your entire hand is visible in the frame
  • Form letters clearly and distinctly

Supported Signs

SignSync currently supports the 26 letters of the ASL alphabet (A-Z). Future updates may include numbers and common words.
